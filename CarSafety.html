How FSD Systems Improve Traffic Safety

Emergency Braking

Applies brakes automatically if a crash is detected.

  • Has faster reaction time than Humans
  • Can apply emergency braking faster than Humans

Blind Spot Monitoring

Warns about vehicles in adjacent lanes outside mirrors.

Helps avoid collisions by alerting drivers when cars approach their blind spots and aren't visible.

Lane Keeping Assistant

Keeps your car centered automatically within the lane.

Detects unintentional drifting and gently corrects steering to maintain lane discipline.

Traffic Sign Recognition

Reads road signs in real-time.

  • Detects speed limits, stop signs, and yield warnings.
  • Helps maintain speed compliance.
  • Improves safety by reading signs even when GPS fails.

Driver Monitoring

Watches driver attention and alertness.

  • Tracks eye movement and head direction.
  • Warns driver if drowsy or distracted.
  • Can slow or stop the car if unresponsive.

Pedestrian Detection

Detects and avoids pedestrians automatically.

  • Uses cameras and radar to detect pedestrians.
  • Warns or stops automatically to avoid them.
  • Works in crosswalks and parking lots.
