Audi's Traffic Jam Pilot

Audi introduced its Traffic Jam Pilot for the 2019 Audi A8 car
and the car is designed to handle slow-moving traffic at speeds up to 60 km/h.
It can drive the car without the need of driver input and allows the driver to focus on other activities.

How does it work?

The traffic jam pilot uses LIDAR (Light Detection and Ranging) via the sensors on the car to determine the location of the vehicle and calculate what actions to take.
