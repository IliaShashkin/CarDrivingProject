Level 1

Driver Assistance

Adaptive Cruise Control (ACC).
                The system controls either steering or
                \acceleration/braking
                (but not both simultaneously).
                The driver must always keep their
                hands on the wheel
                and monitor the road.

Level 2

Partial Automation

                Example: Tesla Autopilot, Lane Keeping Assist (LKA) systems.
The vehicle can simultaneously manage steering and acceleration/braking.
The driver must remain ready to take control at any moment.

Level 3

Conditional Automation

Example: Audi A8 with Traffic Jam Pilot.
The system can take full control in specific conditions (e.g., in traffic jams).
The driver can be distracted but must be ready to intervene if prompted by the system.

FSD

High Automation

    Example: Robotaxi prototypes (Waymo, advanced Tesla FSD Beta versions).
The vehicle can fully drive itself in specific geofenced areas and conditions without driver intervention.
The driver can completely relax, but the system is limited to predefined scenarios.
